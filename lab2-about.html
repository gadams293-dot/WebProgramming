About Lab 2

The purpose of this lab is to train us creating new html pages with consistent naming, proper linking, and proper organization. For this page, we are tasked with adding an h1 header labeling this page, a paragraph describing the purpose of this lab, and tow different links: a relative path back to the lab2-home.html page, and a "mailto:" link allowing an email to be sent. The links must be organized in a navigation table.
